Electric Vehicle⚡

An electric vehicle is any vehicle that is powered by electricity rather than traditional fuel based engine. Electric Vehicles generally provide better fuel efficiency and amount to lower emissions over their entire lifecycle.

Over the recent years, electric vehicles proved to be sufficent for a wider customer base as the development in the EV sector has been making decent progress.

The intention of this project is to provide users with a reference on how the current market will have an impact if they choose either an EV or a conventional Petrol powered vehicle by using various factors such as Fuel price, Initial Cost, Maintenance Cost, Mileage, Average travelling distance.
